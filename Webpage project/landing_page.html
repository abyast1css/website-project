Palowski Skates

Simon Palowski has always had a fasination with skateboarding even at a youngage he would always be out performing tricks at his local skatepark. Eventually he went on to compete in local tournament even winning some awards along his way. This site is to show his love and appreciation for the sport including boards that he either made himself or has partnered with, Tricks and tips so you can become a pro yourself and a events page where you can go and test your skills against others and maybe even meet palowski himself.
